About Me

I am a Jersey boy who found a home in the south. Graphic/Web design are my specialties, people are my inspiration, and passion is my fuel. I am considered a jack of all trades; my skill set that ranges from print to digital end products.

I'm a Savannah College of Art and Design Graduate with over 8 years of industry experience. With my ability to self-start, self-teach, and problem solve I bring a unique perspective to creative environments. I am currently enrolled at the GA Tech Full Stack Bootcamp where I am enhacing my abilities and skills in the digital relam.

I don't feel there is anything I can't do if I want to. I am always looking for the next challenge, adventure, or opportunity. I hope that I get the chance to speak with you and listen to what you have to say.
